## Background
Lee Lorenzen is an extraordinary figure in entrepreneurship with 56 years of startup experience starting at age 10. He was part of the Xerox team that invented the first desktop GUI. He has founded or worked with 32 companies, with 25 of those companies failing (providing hard-earned learnings). His major success was Ventura Publisher, which generated $36 million in profit. He has raised capital from Bill Gates and Jeff Bezos personally. He created the GEM fundraising system which has achieved 42 successful raises since COVID.
